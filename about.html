Our Mission and History

FoodForward SA is a non-profit organization established in 2009 to address widespread hunger and food waste in South Africa. Our core mission is to bridge the gap between food abundance and food insecurity by rescuing quality, surplus food from manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers, and redistributing it to registered beneficiary organizations that serve vulnerable communities.

Our Vision

A South Africa where no person goes hungry, and where food waste is minimized.

Our Impact

Over the past decade, we have grown to be one of the country's largest food redistribution organizations, serving thousands of organizations monthly and touching the lives of nearly a million people. We believe in providing sustainable solutions that empower communities.
