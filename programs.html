Our Core Food Rescue Programs

FoodForward SA runs several key programs to ensure food reaches those who need it most, efficiently and safely.

Food Rescue Program

Our flagship program focuses on collecting surplus edible food from the supply chain and distributing it to community organizations.

* Impact: Focuses on daily distribution and bulk donations.

Virtual Foodbanking (VFB)

An innovative solution allowing corporate partners to donate funds which are converted into specific staple goods for beneficiary organizations via a virtual platform.

* Impact: Expands reach to rural and remote areas.

Mandela Day Food Drive

An annual national campaign encouraging the public and businesses to donate non-perishable food items to replenish our warehouses.

* Impact: Provides a significant annual boost to stock levels.
